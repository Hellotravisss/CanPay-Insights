# Backlink Outreach Targets

Small, relevant backlink outreach batch for CanPay Insights. Use these as manual follow-up records after the first send.

## Primary Ask

Ask editors to add CanPay Insights as a free Canadian take-home pay resource on pages about newcomers, payroll, bookkeeping, HR, or job offer evaluation.

Recommended URLs to pitch:

- https://canpayinsights.ca/
- https://canpayinsights.ca/link-to-canpay
- https://canpayinsights.ca/salary-after-tax-canada
- https://canpayinsights.ca/65000-after-tax-ontario
