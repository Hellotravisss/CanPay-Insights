# CanPay Insights Promotion Playbook

## Positioning

CanPay Insights is a free Canadian payroll calculator for workers comparing take-home pay by province. Lead with:

- Canadian payroll math: federal tax, provincial tax, CPP, EI
- Hourly, annual salary, and timesheet modes
- Province-specific landing pages
- Free to use, with optional cloud save

## Priority Channels

1. Google Search Console
   - Request indexing for `/salary-after-tax-canada`, `/cpp-ei-calculator`, and every province page.
   - Re-submit `/sitemap.xml` after each major content batch.

2. Reddit feedback posts
   - Good-fit communities: `r/PersonalFinanceCanada`, `r/SideProject`, `r/ImmigrationCanada`, local city/province subreddits.
   - Ask for feedback instead of dropping a naked link.

3. LinkedIn
   - Post from a personal profile, not only a product page.
   - Target HR, payroll, bookkeeping, newcomers, and job-search angles.

4. Backlink outreach
   - Use `/link-to-canpay` when emailing bloggers, HR resource pages, newcomer guides, and Canadian personal finance newsletters.

5. Content expansion
   - Add salary-specific pages for common searches: `$50,000 after tax Ontario`, `$60,000 after tax BC`, `$80,000 after tax Alberta`.
   - Add comparison pages: `Ontario vs Alberta take-home pay`, `BC vs Ontario salary after tax`.
